AIcontextJump

Instantly sync your projects with any AI.



Stop wasting time manually copying and pasting files. AIcontextJump instantly generates a structured, LLM-ready snapshot of your entire project, giving your AI total awareness of your codebase, data structure, and logic in seconds.



Why use AIcontextJump?

Instant Clarity: Your AI understands your project structure, coding style, and data schema immediately.



Smart Segmentation: Large projects are automatically split into manageable, token-safe files so you never hit upload limits.



Data-Aware: Automatically extracts headers from CSV/Excel files to provide a "data blueprint" without the bloat of raw data.



Keep Sync’d: Use it at the end of every session to ensure your local work and your AI conversation are always on the same page.



Getting Started

Download: Get the latest AIcontextJump-Setup.exe from AIcontextJump.com.



Install: Run the installer once to set it up on your computer.



Compile: Open the app and select your project folder. It will generate a set of context\_part\_x.txt files inside a dedicated subfolder.



Sync: Upload these context files into your AI (ChatGPT, Gemini, Co-Pilot, etc.). You're now ready to build!



Workflow Tip

Run AIcontextJump at the end of every coding session. This ensures your next AI conversation starts with a perfectly up-to-date, current snapshot of where you left off.
